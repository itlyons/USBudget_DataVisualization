Federal Budget Projection Explorer

This dashboard shows historical federal budget data as well as the Congressional Budget Office's (CBO)
projections for revenue collection, spending, and overall debt.

Click the buttons on the righthand side of the chart to toggle between the CBO's budgetary overview,
their projections for revenue collection, and spending projections.
