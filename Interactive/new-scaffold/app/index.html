Federal Budget Projection Explorer

This dashboard shows historical federal budget data as well as the Congressional Budget Office's (CBO)
projections for revenue collection, spending, and overall debt.

All projections and historic data comes from the Congressional Budget Office.
For the "Overview" view, CBO's January 2019 Long Term Budget Outlook Projections January 2019 Long Term Budget Outlook Projections were used.
"Spending" and "Revenues" data sourced from CBO's 10-Year 2019-2029 Budget and Economic Outlook document was used.
